📧 Communication Strategy

Manage your email communication with participants through the event lifecycle. Use these templates as guides for key communication moments.

📋 Email 1: Poll Invitation
First touchpoint - Gather responses and preferences
INITIAL OUTREACH
Purpose: Introduce the upcoming event and gather RSVPs and venue preferences from participants. This is your first communication about the specific event.
🔑 Critical Elements to Include:
  • Event date(s) - Multiple options if polling for dates
  • Event type/theme - What kind of gathering is this?
  • Participant group composition - Who will be attending (e.g., "8 women + 8 men")
  • Venue options - If multiple venues, include all for voting
  • Response deadline - When do you need answers by?
  • Poll link - Make it easy to respond
  • FAQ link - Answer common questions upfront
  • Tone - Warm, inviting, build excitement
Subject: Let's Plan Our Next Event! 🎉 Dear [Name], We're excited to invite you to our next event! Here's what we need from you: 📅 Date Options: [List dates] 📍 Venue Preferences: [List venues with descriptions] 👥 Group: [Description - e.g., "Mono Long-Term 6W+6M"] Please vote on your preferred date and venue by [DEADLINE]. 🔗 [POLL LINK] Have questions? Check our FAQ: [FAQ LINK] Looking forward to seeing you! [Organizer Name]

✅ Email 2: Confirmation Email
After poll closes - Confirm attendance and next steps
CONFIRMATION
Purpose: Thank confirmed participants, provide finalized event details, explain logistics, and set clear expectations for the event. This solidifies their commitment.
🔑 Critical Elements to Include:
  • Thank you message - Show appreciation for confirming
  • Final attendance count - "We have 8 women and 8 men confirmed"
  • Event team/color - What group/team they're part of
  • Final date & time - No ambiguity about when
  • Final venue - Confirmed location
  • How the event will run - Explain the flow/structure
  • Code of Conduct reference - Link to expectations
  • Important logistics - Transportation, dress code, what to bring
  • Constraint: Can't send someone else - Make clear it's personal attendance only
  • FAQ link - For last-minute questions
  • Contact info - How to reach organizer if issues arise
Subject: Confirmed! ✅ Your Event Details Dear [Name], Thank you for confirming! Happy to see we have 8 women and 8 men confirmed for our event. 🎯 Event Details: Date: [Final Date & Time] Location: [Final Venue] Team: [Team Name/Color] 📝 How This Will Go Down: [Describe event flow, activities, timeline] ⚠️ Important: - This is a personal invitation - you cannot send someone in your place - Please review our Code of Conduct: [LINK] - [Any other logistics/requirements] ❓ Questions? Check our FAQ: [FAQ LINK] Looking forward to seeing you! [Organizer Name] [Contact Info]

📍 Email 3: On the Day Reminder
Day of event - Final reminder and point of contact
EVENT DAY
Purpose: Remind participants of event details on the day, provide point of contact information, and set clear expectations for any activities or questions they may have during the event.
🔑 Critical Elements to Include:
  • Excitement message - Build anticipation for the event
  • Final time & location - As a reminder, make it very clear
  • Point of contact - Who to reach if they're running late or have issues
  • Contact method - Phone number, text, email, etc.
  • Arrival time - When should they arrive?
  • Parking/access info - How to get there and where to park
  • Questions for the date - What you'll be asking or discussing
  • Any last-minute changes - Communicate clearly if something changed
  • Code of Conduct reminder - Brief reference to expectations
Subject: See You Tonight! 🎉 Hi [Name], We're excited! Tonight's the big event. Here's everything you need to know: ⏰ Time: [Time - Arrive by HH:MM] 📍 Location: [Full Address] 🅿️ Parking: [Parking Info] 📞 Point of Contact: [Organizer Name] Phone: [Number] Text/WhatsApp: [Details] ❓ We'll be discussing: [List topics/questions for the date] 💡 Remember to: - Be respectful and follow our Code of Conduct - [Any other reminders] See you soon! [Organizer Name]

📝 Email 4: Feedback & Follow-up
After event - Gather feedback and maintain connection
POST-EVENT
Purpose: Thank participants for attending, gather their feedback to improve future events, and keep them engaged with the community. This is your chance to learn and build loyalty.
🔑 Critical Elements to Include:
  • Thank you message - Express genuine appreciation for their attendance
  • Event highlights - Remind them of positive moments
  • Feedback form link - Make it easy to share thoughts
  • Specific questions - Ask about: venue, vibe, activities, logistics, would they attend similar events?
  • Appreciation for specific contributions - If they helped, acknowledge it
  • Upcoming events hint - Build excitement for next gathering
  • Optional: Social proof - Share photos/testimonials from event
  • Deadline for feedback - When you need responses by
  • Contact for any concerns - If they have issues to discuss
Subject: Thank You for an Amazing Event! 🙌 Dear [Name], Thank you so much for joining us! We had an incredible time with you and the rest of the group. ✨ Highlights from the event: [Mention memorable moments or connections made] 📋 We'd love your feedback! Your input helps us plan even better events. Please take 5 minutes to share your thoughts: [FEEDBACK FORM LINK] Questions on the form: - How did you like the venue? - What was the vibe like for you? - Would you attend a similar event? - Any suggestions for improvement? 🎉 We're already planning our next gathering. If you have ideas or want to help, let us know! [Contact info for follow-up] Thanks again, [Organizer Name]
