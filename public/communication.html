📧 Communication Strategy

Manage your email communication with participants through the event lifecycle. Edit templates as needed and check them off before sending.

📋 Email 1: Poll Invitation
First touchpoint - Gather responses and preferences
INITIAL OUTREACH
Purpose: Introduce the upcoming event and gather RSVPs and venue preferences from participants. This is your first communication about the specific event.
📝 HTML Email Code

Let's Plan Our Next Event! 🎉

Dear [Name],

We're excited to invite you to our next event! Here's what we need from you:

📅 Date Options:
[List dates]

📍 Venue Preferences:
[List venues with descriptions]

👥 Group:
[Description - e.g., "Mono Long-Term 6W+6M"]

Please vote on your preferred date and venue by [DEADLINE].

Vote Now →

Have questions? Check our FAQ: FAQ

Looking forward to seeing you!

✅ Email 2: Confirmation Email
After poll closes - Confirm attendance and next steps
CONFIRMATION
Purpose: Thank confirmed participants, provide finalized event details, explain logistics, and set clear expectations for the event. This solidifies their commitment.
📝 HTML Email Code

✅ You're Confirmed!

Dear [Name],

Thank you for confirming! 🎉 Happy to see we have 8 women and 8 men confirmed for our event.

🎯 Event Details:
Date: [Final Date & Time]
Location: [Final Venue]
Team: [Team Name/Color]

📝 How This Will Go Down:
[Describe event flow, activities, timeline]

⚠️ Important:

  • This is a personal invitation - you cannot send someone in your place
  • Please review our Code of Conduct: [LINK]
  • [Any other logistics/requirements]

❓ Questions? Check our FAQ

Looking forward to seeing you!
[Organizer Name]

📍 Email 3: On the Day Reminder
Day of event - Final reminder and point of contact
EVENT DAY
Purpose: Remind participants of event details on the day, provide point of contact information, and set clear expectations for any activities or questions they may have during the event.
📝 HTML Email Code

See You Tonight! 🎉

Hi [Name],

We're excited! Tonight's the big event. Here's everything you need to know:

⏰ Time: [Time - Arrive by HH:MM]
📍 Location: [Full Address]
🅿️ Parking: [Parking Info]
📞 Point of Contact: [Organizer Name]
Phone: [Number]
Text/WhatsApp: [Details]

❓ We'll be discussing:
[List topics/questions for the date]

💡 Remember to:

  • Be respectful and follow our Code of Conduct
  • [Any other reminders]

See you soon!

📝 Email 4: Feedback & Follow-up
After event - Gather feedback and maintain connection
POST-EVENT
Purpose: Thank participants for attending, gather their feedback to improve future events, and keep them engaged with the community. This is your chance to learn and build loyalty.
📝 HTML Email Code

Thank You for an Amazing Event! 🙌

Dear [Name],

Thank you so much for joining us! We had an incredible time with you and the rest of the group.

✨ Highlights from the event:
[Mention memorable moments or connections made]

📋 We'd love your feedback!

Your input helps us plan even better events. Please take 5 minutes to share your thoughts:

Share Your Feedback →

Questions on the form:

  • How did you like the venue?
  • What was the vibe like for you?
  • Would you attend a similar event?
  • Any suggestions for improvement?

🎉 We're already planning our next gathering. If you have ideas or want to help, let us know!

[Contact info for follow-up]

Thanks again,
[Organizer Name]
